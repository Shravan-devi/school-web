Victorious Kidss Educares

Victorious Kidss Educares (VKE) is an IB World School located in Kharadi, Pune, India. It was established by Dr. Robbin Ghosh in January 1997. The school is driven by the motto of ‘Learning to Love to Learn’, and strives to create ‘lifelong learners’. It is a co-educational school from Playgroup up to Grade 12th.

  • Two VKE Stars Shine in ISRO Cyberspace essay Competition

    Saloni Maniar, a student of DP (Std.11) and Simran Pasankar, MYP student (Std. 10) have won an entry with their essays in the top 11-500 entries at the national competition held by ISRO. They have also received a certificate each from ISRO. ISRO conducted cyberspace competition in total four competitions in eight categories for students of class 1 to 12 through online mode. The competition was conducted by ICC -2020 in the month of July & August 2020. A total of 2,04,631 participants have taken part in the competition.
